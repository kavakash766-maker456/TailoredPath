Our Mission

Built for the Modern
Job Seeker

ResumeAI was created to eliminate the hours candidates spend manually tailoring applications — and level the playing field with anyone who has access to a professional resume writer.

The Problem We Solve

The modern job market is ruthless. Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) automatically screen out 75% of resumes before a human ever reads them — often because the resume lacks the exact keywords and phrases the employer searched for.

Top candidates pay $200–$500 for a professional resume writer to tailor each application. Everyone else sends generic resumes and wonders why they never hear back.

ResumeAI changes that. By combining state-of-the-art language models (Meta's Llama 3.3 70B, running on Groq's lightning-fast inference infrastructure) with expert-level resume writing prompts, we give every job seeker the same advantage as a professional career coach.

What We Stand For

Privacy First

Your resume data is sent directly to Groq's API. We never store, log, or sell your personal information.

Lightning Fast

Groq's custom LPU hardware runs Llama 3.3 70B at speeds that make other inference providers feel slow. Results in seconds, not minutes.

Expert Quality

Our prompts are engineered around real hiring manager and ATS best practices — STAR method, quantified achievements, keyword mirroring.

Open & Transparent

We use your own Groq API key. You can verify exactly what data is sent — it goes directly from your browser to Groq. No middleman.

Accessible

Groq offers a free API tier. We want anyone — regardless of budget — to have access to professional-quality career tools.

Developer Friendly

Built with pure HTML, Tailwind CSS, and Vanilla JS — zero frameworks, zero build tools. Easy to self-host, fork, and customize.
