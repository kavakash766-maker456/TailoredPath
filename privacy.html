Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how ResumeAI ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our web application at this website (the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how the tool works.

The short version: We do not collect, store, or share your personal data. Your resume content goes directly from your browser to Groq's API. That's it.

1. Information We Do NOT Collect

ResumeAI is a client-side tool. We do not operate a backend server that processes or stores your data. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
  • Your resume content or job description text
  • Your Groq API key (it is stored only in your browser's sessionStorage)
  • IP addresses or device fingerprints
  • Usage analytics or behavioral tracking data
  • Cookies for tracking purposes

2. How the Tool Works (Data Flow)

When you click "Tailor My Application," the following happens entirely within your browser:

  • Your job description text and resume content are read from the on-page text areas
  • A request is constructed and sent directly from your browser to the Groq API endpoint (https://api.groq.com)
  • Groq returns a response, which is displayed in your browser
  • No data passes through any ResumeAI server — because no such server exists

This architecture means your sensitive resume data is only ever transmitted between your device and Groq. To understand how Groq handles data, please review Groq's Privacy Policy.

3. API Keys

Your Groq API key is stored in sessionStorage in your browser, which means:

  • It is cleared automatically when you close the browser tab
  • It is never transmitted to our servers (we have none)
  • It is only sent to api.groq.com as part of authenticated API requests
  • Other tabs or websites cannot access your sessionStorage

We strongly recommend never sharing your API key with others and revoking keys you no longer need at console.groq.com.

4. Local Storage and Cookies

ResumeAI does not set any persistent cookies or use localStorage for tracking. We use sessionStorage solely to temporarily hold your Groq API key for the duration of your browser session.

5. Third-Party Services

The Service loads the following third-party resources:

  • Google Fonts — for typography (Syne, DM Sans). Google may log font requests; see Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Tailwind CSS CDN — a CSS framework loaded from a CDN. No tracking.
  • Groq API — processes your text inputs when you submit a request. Subject to Groq's Privacy Policy.

6. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us immediately.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us.
